Find Your Path

About:

This is a tool that can be used to help you find a potential career path based on your interests, skills, and personality. It works by providing a response that is based on what is shared. The tool is open to be used in whatever way your heart desires.

Instructions (example of how to use):

1. To receive your personalized career recommendation: complete the game you are directed to after pressing the button labeled 'Explore Interests'

2. Once you have completed the first step: Click on the button 'Learn More'. Feel free to ask questions regarding the best career for you and the types of jobs that exist in that career. If you want to learn more about specific jobs, feel free to do that as well. The tool is open to your thoughts as well; so, if you dislike a part of a job, then share that.

3. Have fun exploring!
